What is colour?

According to Google, "colour" refers to: 

...the property possessed by an object of producing different sensations on the eye as a result of the way the object reflects or emits light.

Math and Colour

Mathematicians can solve equations to figure out ways colours can be combined. A colour space refers to a specific combination of colours. A colour model is an abstract, mathematical model that describes the ways that colours can be represented as tuples (e.g. threein RGB, or four in CMYK).

How We Match Your Clothes

Matching colours is hard. There's a bajillion and a half ways to go about it, but here's a brief outline of how we did it.

  1. Collect a list of all clothing colours
  2. Generate a list of all compatible colours to each clothing colour
  3. Search through clothing colours for matches or close matches to compatible colours
  4. Check for item compatibility (tops with bottoms only, no tops with tops)
  5. Render colour co-ordinated clothing matches
