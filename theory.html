Some Color Theory

What is colour?

According to Google, "colour" refers to: 

...the property possessed by an object of producing different sensations on the eye as a result of the way the object reflects or emits light.

Math and Colour

Mathematicians can solve equations to figure out ways colours can be combined. A colour space refers to a specific combination of colours. A colour model is an abstract, mathematical model that describes the ways that colours can be represented as tuples (e.g. threein RGB, or four in CMYK).
